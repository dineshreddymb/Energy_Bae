ENERGY BAE - SOLAR CALCULATOR

How it works:
User uploads an electricity bill (PDF or image) to the Streamlit app. The app sends the bill to n8n workflow which processes it using Groq AI. The AI extracts all billing information from the bill and analyzes the customer's electricity consumption. Based on the analysis, it calculates the recommended solar system capacity and number of panels needed. Results including customer details, monthly consumption, bill amount, and solar recommendations are displayed on the dashboard with an option to download a detailed Excel report.
