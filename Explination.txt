Energy Bae is an AI-powered solar calculator Streamlit app that analyzes electricity bills to provide personalized solar energy recommendations. Users upload their electricity bill (PDF or image), and the app uses n8n workflow with Groq AI to extract billing details like monthly units consumed, bill amount, sanctioned load, and tariff category. The AI processes the bill data and recommends the optimal solar capacity (in kW) and number of solar panels needed to meet the customer's energy requirements. The app displays comprehensive results including daily average consumption, predicted next month's bill savings, and downloadable Excel reports. The entire workflow is automated through a webhook integration that communicates between the Streamlit frontend and n8n backend AI processing engine.
