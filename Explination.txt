ENERGY BAE - SOLAR CALCULATOR

Energy Bae is a Streamlit web app that analyzes electricity bills using AI to recommend solar installations.

KEY POINTS:
- Users upload electricity bills (PDF/Image)
- Groq AI extracts 9+ data points from the bill
- Displays: customer name, units consumed, bill amount, tariff, recommended solar capacity, panel count
- Generates downloadable Excel reports
- Powered by n8n workflow + Groq API backend
